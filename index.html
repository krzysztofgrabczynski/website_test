FOR TRIPS

Sign up and add a trip anywhere

Simply set up a trip, select the country you’re traveling to and personalize it with one of our default images. It’s all about making your trip uniquely yours!

FOR EXPENSES

Track your trip expenses

Easily add expenses to your trip, pick categories, and check your travel stats anytime to see how much you’ve spent in each area!

FOR DETAIL VIEW

Switch to detailed view

Use the toggle button to switch from the photo view of your trips to a compact layout, focusing solely on expense details without images.

FOR STATISTICS

Check your travel stats

View your full travel history in one place. See how many trips you’ve completed, count the countries you’ve explored, and review your total travel spending!

Features

CRUD

Perform CRUD operations on trips and expenses in a simple and intuitive way.

Pick trip image

Choose from 15 default images to represent their trips. Each image is carefully selected to capture a variety of travel themes and destinations.

Check statistics

See overall stats, total number of trips, countries visited, spending and access detailed stats per trip, showing expenses by category for a deeper look at where the budget goes.

Detailed view

Check all your trips in a more detailed view for a clearer overview of your travel details and expenses with just one button.

Choose category

Select categories from several basic options, such as transport, health, or accommodation. This helps in organizing your expenses more effectively.

Choose currency

Select the currency in which your expenses are displayed. Additionally, you can enter expenses in any currency, and they will automatically convert to your app’s main currency for a consistent overview.

Register via Google/Apple account

Log in or register via your Google or Apple account to make the process quicker and more convenient.

Cloud sync

Access your trips anytime, from any device. Your data is securely stored, ensuring that your travel plans are always up-to-date and easily accessible wherever you are

❤️ Who we are

We are a team of young, passionate developers and avid travelers who created Travel Balance out of our love for both programming and exploring the world.

🌎 Our Inspiration

As travelers ourselves, we wanted to build a tool that would not only help us track and manage our expenses during our trips but also provide others with an easy and efficient way to handle their travel finances.

🎯 Our Goal

Our goal is to offer a practical solution for managing finances across different countries, helping travelers stay organized and stress-free, no matter where they are.
